## Primary Data Sources Used

1. **Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) - https://www.cdc.gov**
   - General reference for U.S. disease surveillance methodology and reporting standards
   - Disease classification and epidemiological parameters (R0 values, typical incidence patterns)
   - Framework for understanding national disease tracking systems

2. **New York State Department of Health - MMR Vaccination Rates**
   - **URL**: https://www.health.ny.gov/diseases/communicable/measles/vaccine/
   - **Data Used**: MMR vaccination rates by county in New York State (2025 data)

3. **WHO Immunization Data Portal - Region of the Americas**
   - **URL**: https://immunizationdata.who.int/dashboard/regions/region-of-the-americas
   - **Data Used**: Regional immunization coverage trends and vaccine-preventable disease surveillance framework
   - **Purpose**: Verified standard vaccine names (MMR, DTaP/Tdap, BCG, etc.) and immunization program structures
   - **Application**: Confirmed vaccine-disease relationships for database schema design

4. **CDC National Notifiable Diseases Surveillance System (NNDSS)**
   - **Reference**: Annual summaries of notifiable infectious diseases in the United States

5. **CDC Vaccine Information Statements (VIS)**
   - **Reference**: Official vaccine documentation for healthcare providers
   - **Purpose**: Confirmed standard vaccine names and targeted diseases
